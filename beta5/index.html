The value of data 

 

Why Rubik?

Deriving value from data is crucial for maintaining a competitive edge in your industry. But, the technical acumen required to funnel the right data to the right person at the right time is a barrier to becoming a data-driven enterprise.

In the past, only those with high tech skills were able to access and understand the complexities of massive datasets. As a result, extracting the data’s full value was limited.

Rubik solves the data gatekeeper problem. We build data products with a streamlined and powerful UX so that anyone in your enterprise can easily glean valuable insights from a variety of data types -- without the need for advanced technical knowledge.
